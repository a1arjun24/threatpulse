Subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel, go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone and select ThreatPulse. You can cancel anytime and will retain Pro access until the end of the billing period.
ThreatPulse pulls CVE data from the NIST National Vulnerability Database (NVD) for the last 90 days. If no CVEs appear, it means there are no published vulnerabilities for that vendor in the last 90 days — which is actually good news. Try pulling down to refresh on the My Stack screen to fetch the latest data.
ThreatPulse stores your vendor stack, preferences, and confirmed-patched CVEs locally on your device only. We do not collect personal data, run analytics, or store anything on our servers. See our full Privacy Policy for details.
